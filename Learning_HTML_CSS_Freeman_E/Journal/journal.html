On a scooter across the United States..

A diary of my travels on my own scooter across the United States!

20 August, 2012

So, I've already driven 1,200 miles and been to several interesting places: Walla Walla, Washington, Magic City, Idaho, Bountiful, Utah, Last Chance, Colorado, Wye, Arizona, Trough-or-Concequence, New Mexico.

July 14, 2012

I've seen a couple of Burma Shave-style signs on the side of the road:

"If you don't see cars passing by, they might hit you. One moment and infinity.."

I definitely didn't want to be hit by a car!

June 2, 2012:

The first day of my trip! I can't believe I was finally able to put things aside and go on my trip. Since I was going to ride my scooter, I couldn't take many things with me: just my cell phone, iPod, digital camera, and candy bar. Just the essentials. As Lao Tzu would say, A journey of a thousand miles begins with one step to the scooter.
