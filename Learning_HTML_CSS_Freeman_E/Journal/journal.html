June 2, 2012:

The first day of my trip! I can't believe I was finally able to put things aside and go on my trip. Since I was going to ride my scooter, I couldn't take many things with me: just my..

  • cell phone,
  • iPod,
  • digital camera,

Just the essentials. As Lao Tzu would say, A journey of a thousand miles begins with one step to the scooter.
